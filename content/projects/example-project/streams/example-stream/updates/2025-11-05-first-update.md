# First Update

Welcome! This is your first update entry. Updates are how you document your daily progress, experiments, observations, and milestones.

## What to Include

Each update can contain:
- **Experiments**: Document tests, procedures, and results
- **Observations**: Record what you noticed or learned
- **Milestones**: Celebrate achievements and completed phases
- **Notes**: General thoughts, ideas, or reflections
